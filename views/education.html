2018 - 2019

Télécom Paris

Master 2 | DATA & KNOWLEDGE

PARIS, France

The Data & Knowledge program is a second year master program in computer science. It is concerned with:

  • Knowledge & Semantics: knowledge bases, NLP, information extraction
  • Machine learning + Deep learning + Recommendation system (factorization-based)
  • Databases: NoSQL (Neo4J, Pig, Redis), big data frameword (Hadoop), streaming applications
  • Web data management

The curriculum brings together a variety of subjects from the fields of data management, knowledge management, knowledge engineering, machine learning, and data mining. Topics include system design and architecture, storage, indexing and optimization, data analytics, knowledge representation and reasoning, semantic interoperability, and data mining, all with a special focus on processing very large amounts of data.

2017 - 2018

École Polytechnique

Master 1 | DATA SCIENCE

PALAISEAU, France

The Jacques Herbrand Masters programmes are elite postgraduate programmes in Computer Science at University Paris-Saclay, proposed by Ecole Polytechnique, ENS Cachan and Université Paris-Sud. One of the specificities of the Jacques Herbrand Masters is to devote a significant part of their programmes to research, whether as a preparation to a career in academia or as the source of industrial innovation.

The Data Science branch equips the students with a solid background necessary for advanced M2 studies in the area of AI and Bigdata Management with a large range of real world applications. The courses include:

  • Machine learning & Text mining
  • Artificial intelligence: NLP, reinforcement learning, deep learning
  • Math (regression, statisticd)
  • Advanced database management
  • Big data

Bachelor | COMPUTER SCIENCE

HO CHI MINH, Vietnam

This year was rather focused on the theory of Computer Science. It complements the technical skills I have acquired after two first years of the DUT.

The topics include:
  • Programming: object-oriented (Java), client-serveur architecture (PHP)
  • Algorithmic

This program was the collaboration between University of Pierre and Marie Curie (France) and French University Pole (Vietnam). The goal was to let students in Vietnam access to high-quality undergraduate programs of France while staying in Vietnam. The diploma are delivered by University of Pierre and Marie Curie.
