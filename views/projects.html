LTCI, Télécom Paris

March 2018 - August 2019

The new algorithms are to be integrated in MOA (Massive Online Analysis), a software environment designed for implementing and running experiments on online, evolving data streams.

ConnectionLens:
Connecting the dots across heterogeneous data sources

Link prediction in citation network

École Polytechnique

March 2018

A dataset of scientific articles is given in form of a graph, where nodes are the articles and edges are the citation of one article to another. Some of the edges are missing

The aim is to find those missing links using different types of data (graphs, raw texts, numbers). We have to use various techniques of:

  • text mining: text similarity, word embedding, etc.
  • machine learning: SVM, random forest, deep learning, etc.
to recover the missing links.

The report of the project can be found here.

Wireless Sensor Network - Petri Net (WSN-PN)

Pôle Universitaire Français &
University of Technology of Vietnam

April 2016 - July 2016

WSN's are an emerging technology of the network domain that uses low-cost wireless devices to provide high-resolution sensing of the environment. However, a congestion on the network may make a node dysfunctional.

WSN-PN is a software that modelizes a real-world sensor networks, detects and prevents congestion. My role is to integrate the probability of congestion for searching paths in WSN-PN. The report is found here.
